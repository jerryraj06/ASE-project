Where Communication is versatile

It is one of a kind website, providing communication between peers working across various companies

Welcome to theICIP:International Company InteractionPortal

Our organisation has come up with a convenient yet versatile portal for communication for peers working on a similar project within their company or different companies which are not related to each other

Features

User Research

The users(employees) are thoroughly researched before making them a part of the portal.

Data Collection

Sophisticated and genuine information is extracted.Unwanted content is discouraged.

Profile

Profiles created by a user are secured safely and the data required is properly analysed

Development

The features of this website is to improve the accessibility of employees working in different companies in form of message,text or videochat

Quality

The quality of information stored for each company is filtered and only reliable information source is taken for website consideration.

Release

Information released is also very safe and proficient and confirmed befor release.

About ICIP

ICIP Stands for International Company Interaction Portal.

ICIP allows employees in higher posts such as companies to take authrity and place employees at a lower rank in projects.

ICIP allows encrypted chat messaging and vieo conferencing among people within the same company or working in different companies .

  • It provides vesatility and proper verification.
  • It allows ease of access between employees
  • It searches among the companies to give recognition for employees for who belongs where.
  • Each company has a profile along with n individual profile as well.
  • Company information is secured so it wont be displayed unless you are logged in with only permission

Amazing free bootstrap template

Interaction can happen as follows in this website which is versatile and accessible to all who have been confirmed as employees of the companies registered on the website.

  • Messaging between two people within the same company
  • Messaging among people belonging to different companies
  • Video call between two people belonging to same company
  • Video conferencing between people belonging to different companies
  • Encryption of the messages which are sent during a chat (private)

These above represents how interaction occurs now let us discuss about project handling for an individual company in website/portal.

Project Listing

Projects are listed according to the current company situation and are further added according to the company suggestions and their permission.It is very easy to add and to remove projects in this website ue to ease of interaction.

Project Mangagement

Projects are managed by the higher rank employees (a manager) and they can distribute and select a particular employee to be part of a project accordingly.

Project Exit

An individual can quit any project at any point of time if he does not want it and any manager or higher employee can shift the employees working on the project to another project through informing him.
